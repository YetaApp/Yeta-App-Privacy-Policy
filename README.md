## 2. Information Collection and Use

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

The Application automatically collects certain information when you download and use it. This information may include:

- **Device Information:**
  - Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  - Device model and manufacturer
  - Operating system version
  - Unique device identifiers
  - Mobile network information

- **Usage Information:**
  - The pages of the Application that you visit
  - The time and date of your visit
  - The time spent on those pages
  - The time spent on the Application
  - Features you access and actions you take

### 2.2 Location Information

The Application collects your device's location data to provide the following services:

- **Geolocation Services:** We utilize location data to provide features such as:
  - Personalized content and recommendations
  - Location-based services (e.g., finding nearby stores)
  - Delivery address suggestions
  - Store locator functionality

- **Analytics and Improvements:** Aggregated and anonymized location data helps us:
  - Analyze user behavior and preferences
  - Identify trends and patterns
  - Improve the overall performance and functionality of the Application
  - Enhance user experience

- **Third-Party Services:** Periodically, we may transmit anonymized location data to external services that assist us in:
  - Enhancing the Application
  - Optimizing our offerings
  - Providing location-based features

**Note:** Location data is only collected when you grant permission and is used solely for the purposes stated above. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

### 2.3 Camera and Media Access

The Application requests camera permission for the following purposes:

- **Profile Photos:** Taking photos for your user profile
- **Product Photos:** Uploading photos of products for listings or reviews
- **QR Code Scanning:** Scanning QR codes for payments or product information
- **Document Scanning:** Scanning documents for verification purposes

**Note:** Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to use camera features. We do not access your camera without your explicit action.

### 2.4 Storage Access

The Application requests storage permission to:

- **File Saving:** Save files you create within the app (e.g., receipts, invoices)
- **Data Export:** Export data (e.g., Excel sheets, order history) to your device
- **Image Upload:** Access images from your device to upload to the Application

**Note:** We only access files you explicitly choose to save or upload. We do not access your entire storage without your permission.

### 2.5 Personally Identifiable Information

For a better experience, while using the Application, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
- Profile information

The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

### 2.6 Communication

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with:

- Important information about your account or orders
- Required notices and updates
- Marketing promotions (with your consent, which you can withdraw at any time)
- Customer support communications

## 7. Your Rights Under Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

1. **Right to Access:** You have the right to request access to your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
3. **Right to Erasure:** You have the right to request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
4. **Right to Restrict Processing:** You have the right to request restriction of processing of your data
5. **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
6. **Right to Object:** You have the right to object to processing of your personal data
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent:** You have the right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **yetashopping@gmail.com**. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by law.
